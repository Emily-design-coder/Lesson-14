Dogs
Dogs are types of mammels.
Fun fact: A dog's nose print is as unique as a human's fingerprint!

There are many breeds of dogs such as:
Huskies
A strong, working dog breed from the Arctic, known for sled-pulling (e.g., Siberian Husky).
"Huskies are in a league of their own. They're like cans of energy drink come to life"
(Joseph Robertia).
Labrador retrievers
A popular, medium-to-large, friendly, and intelligent dog breed known for its versatility as a family pet, service dog, and working canine, originally bred in Newfoundland, Canada, to help fishermen.

Abbreviations

There are many abbrevaiations for different dog breeds such as:

GSD for German Shepherd, ACD for Australian Cattle Dog
